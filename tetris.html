How to Play Tetris

Control falling blocks called Tetriminos, rotate and position them to create complete horizontal lines. When a line is completed, it disappears, and you earn points. The game speeds up as you progress, testing your quick thinking and spatial awareness.

Game Features

  • Classic Tetris gameplay mechanics
  • Responsive controls for both desktop and mobile
  • Progressive difficulty levels
  • Score tracking and personal best records

Tips for Success

Plan your moves ahead, keep the stack low, and always leave space for long pieces. Practice T-spins and quick drops to maximize your score. Remember to use the preview window to prepare for upcoming pieces.
